VEXED!

Privacy Policy

The short version

VEXED! does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. What happens on your device stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. VEXED! has no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting service, and no network connection of any kind. The game operates entirely offline.

Data stored on your device

VEXED! stores the following locally using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism:

· Your high scores per difficulty level
· Your chosen difficulty and display preferences (e.g. Arcade Mode)
· Whether the "Rare words" toggle is on or off

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-party SDKs

VEXED! uses no third-party frameworks, advertising SDKs, analytics libraries, or social media integrations.

Children

VEXED! is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no data whatsoever, there are no special considerations regarding children's privacy.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date below.
