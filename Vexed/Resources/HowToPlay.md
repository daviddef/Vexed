# How to Play VEXED!

## The Basics

Letters sit on a grid. Tap a tile to select it, then swipe in any direction — the tile slides until it hits a wall or another tile.

When tiles line up to spell a valid word (left→right or top→bottom), a **gold outline** appears around that word. Tap any tile inside the outline to collect it. The tiles vanish and your score goes up.

That's the whole game. The twist is the vowels.

---

## The Vowel Rule

If **3 or more of the same vowel touch** — horizontally or vertically, in any shape — they all vanish instantly. No warning. No score.

The board edge pulses with that vowel's colour when a cluster is growing close to 3:

- **Red** — A  
- **Green** — E  
- **Blue** — I  
- **Orange** — O  
- **Purple** — U  

Slide one of those vowels away before the cluster reaches 3.

---

## Scoring

| Word length | Base points |
|---|---|
| 3 letters | 30 pts |
| 4 letters | 40 pts |
| 5 letters | 70 pts ✦ |
| 6 letters | 80 pts |
| 7 letters | 90 pts |
| +1 letter | +10 pts |

✦ A bonus kicks in at 5 letters — longer words score disproportionately more than stacking short ones.

### Combo Multiplier

Score words back-to-back without losing vowels and your multiplier climbs:

| Consecutive words | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| 1 | ×1.0 |
| 2 | ×1.5 |
| 3 | ×2.0 |
| 4+ | ×3.0 |

Losing vowels or running out of moves resets the combo to zero.

---

## Tile Forge

Every word you collect forges new tiles into empty spaces on the board. Longer words forge more — this is how you keep the board alive.

### Standard Mode

**Forge tiles = word length − difficulty threshold** (no cap, keeps increasing)

| Word length | Easy (−1) | Medium/Fill (−2) | Hard (−3) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3 letters | +2 | +1 | — |
| 4 letters | +3 | +2 | +1 |
| 5 letters | +4 | +3 | +2 |
| 6 letters | +5 | +4 | +3 |
| 7 letters | +6 | +5 | +4 |

### Kid Mode

Every word gives a **flat bonus** regardless of length:

| Age tier | Forge bonus per word |
|---|---|
| 🌟 Little (5–7) | +4 tiles |
| 🔍 Explorer (8–10) | +3 tiles |
| ⚡ Challenger (11+) | standard formula |

Forged tiles flash white when they appear. They're weighted toward letters that are under-represented on the board.

---

## Word Strip

The strip at the bottom shows every word scoreable **right now** without moving a single tile — sorted longest-first.

- **Tap a chip** to highlight those tiles on the board and see the definition.
- **Tap a highlighted tile** on the board to collect that word.
- **Tap any chip in the history strip** (bottom row) to look up a word you already scored.

---

## Game Over

The game ends when no slide can form a word, even looking two moves ahead. You'll see a grade based on how much of the board's total potential score you captured:

| Grade | Score captured |
|---|---|
| S | 90%+ |
| A | 75–89% |
| B | 60–74% |
| C | 45–59% |
| D | 30–44% |
| F | Below 30% |

## Difficulty Levels

### Easy — 5×5 grid
- 20,000 everyday English words
- Minimum word length: 3 letters
- Forge threshold: −1 (3-letter words give +2 tiles)
- Best for learning the mechanics

### Medium — 7×7 grid
- 62,000 words
- Minimum word length: 3 letters
- Forge threshold: −2 (3-letter words give +1 tile)
- The intended experience

### Hard — 9 cols × screen height
- 62,000 words by default (toggle Rare Words for 132,000)
- Minimum word length: **4 letters** (3-letter words don't count)
- Forge threshold: −3 (need 4+ letters to forge anything)
- Chain long words or the board slowly starves

### Fill — 7 cols × screen height
- Same 62,000-word dictionary as Medium
- Minimum word length: 3 letters
- Forge threshold: −2 (same as Medium)
- Rows are calculated to pack your screen — more tiles, more chaos

## Kid Mode

Turn on **Kid Mode** in the ☰ menu to unlock a friendlier version of VEXED! designed for younger players. Choose an age tier — each one adjusts the rules:

### 🌟 Little (5–7)
- Minimum word length: **2 letters** (short words count!)
- Tile forge: **flat +4 tiles** per word, every time
- Auto-hints: gold glow after 8s idle, tap beacon after 18s
- Dictionary: 20,000 kid-friendly words + all 2-letter Scrabble words (AM, BE, GO, HI, IN, ME, NO, SO, WE…)

### 🔍 Explorer (8–10)
- Minimum word length: 3 letters
- Tile forge: **flat +3 tiles** per word
- Auto-hints: gold glow after 15s idle, tap beacon after 30s
- Dictionary: 20,000 everyday English words

### ⚡ Challenger (11+)
- Minimum word length: 3 letters
- Tile forge: standard formula (same as adult Easy)
- No auto-hints
- Dictionary: 62,000 words — full adult dictionary

In Kid Mode the board size picker changes to **Small / Medium / Full** (same grids as Easy / Medium / Fill). Hard is replaced by Full screen.

### Auto-hints

If the player hasn't moved for a while, a word that can be collected right now gets a **bright pulsing gold border** on each of its tiles. Wait a little longer and a **tap beacon** (expanding rings + hand icon) appears on the first tile — showing exactly where to tap to score it. Any interaction immediately resets the hint.

---

## Settings (☰ menu)

**Kid Mode** — Friendlier rules for younger players: shorter word minimums, flat forge bonuses, and auto-hints.

**Arcade Mode** — Vivid cosmic background, textured tiles, bold scoreboard with coloured stat pills. A complete visual upgrade.

**Rare & archaic words** — Adds technical terms, abbreviations and non-English words to the dictionary. Off by default to keep the word list familiar.

---

## Tips

- **Think before you slide.** Tiles move fast; vowels vanish faster.
- **Check the word strip first.** There may already be words to collect before you move anything.
- **Spread vowels deliberately.** Two Es side by side is fine. Three is catastrophe.
- **Longer words pay double.** A 6-letter word scores roughly as much as two 3-letter words *and* forges far more tiles.
- **Combos matter most on Hard.** A ×3 multiplier on a 7-letter word is your path to an S grade.
- **Tap word chips to look up definitions.** History chips (bottom strip) are tappable too.
- **Kid Mode tip:** if you're stuck, just wait — the hint will show you a word you can tap right now.
